About The Organization

Who is Planeterra?

The world's leading NGO dedicated to turning travel into impact through community-based tourism

Organization Overview

Planeterra is an international non-profit organization founded with the vision of using tourism as a powerful tool for sustainable development. Based in Toronto, Canada, the organization works globally to ensure that local communities benefit directly from tourism.

Registered as a Not-for-Profit under Canada's Act and holding 501(c)3 status in the United States, Planeterra has established itself as the leading voice for community-based tourism worldwide.

Key Focus Areas

  • Empowering women-led tourism enterprises
  • Supporting youth employment in tourism
  • Preserving Indigenous cultures and traditions
  • Developing rural community tourism
  • Environmental protection through tourism
  • Climate and biodiversity initiatives

Mission Statement

"To empower local communities by helping them create and manage tourism enterprises that generate social, cultural, and economic benefits ‚Äî ensuring the ripple effects of tourism are endless."

Strategic Recommendations

Ideas to Improve Digital Marketing

01

Launch TikTok & Reels Strategy

Create short-form video content showcasing day-in-the-life of community tourism enterprises, behind-the-scenes of projects, and inspiring travel moments. Target Gen Z and Millennials who value purpose-driven travel.

Social Media
02

Travel Influencer Partnerships

Partner with travel influencers and content creators for authentic storytelling. Create an "Ambassador Program" for travelers who visit Planeterra projects to share their experiences.

Partnerships
03

Interactive Virtual Tours

Develop 360¬∞ virtual tours of community tourism projects. Allow potential donors and travelers to experience the impact before visiting, increasing emotional connection and donations.

SEO & Content Marketing
