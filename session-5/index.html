Channel 1

Google Search Ads

Three keyword strategies to intercept high-awareness searches and position Planeterra as the answer for conscious travelers.

Competitor Keywords

When users search for organizations similar to Planeterra, our ads appear as an alternative — capturing interest at the moment of exploration.

crooked trails sustainable travel international intrepid travel responsible tourism cares local alike pack for a purpose v social foundation responsible travel com

General Topic Keywords

Broad and phrase-match keywords for users exploring sustainable travel concepts — reaching our audience before they know about us.

eco tourism sustainable tourism community based tourism responsible tourism ethical travel travel with purpose volunteer travel impact travel

Long-Tail Keywords

Question-based and niche queries that signal genuine interest — these drive the most qualified awareness traffic.

how to travel and help local communities best eco tourism organizations to support travel that makes a difference best responsible travel charities

Languages English, French

Skippable In-Stream Ads (15s)

Play before travel vlogs — users see Planeterra's community impact story before their chosen content. Targets travel & sustainability channels.

🔎

Discovery Ads

Appear in YouTube search results when users search "sustainable travel tips," "eco tourism," or "community tourism." Captures research intent.

⚡

6-Second Bumper Ads

Non-skippable micro-ads for pure brand awareness — Planeterra logo + "Travel With Impact" before travel documentaries.
