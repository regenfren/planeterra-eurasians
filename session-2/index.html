Strategic Recommendations

Actionable improvements for each journey stage

üéØ Awareness: Expand Social Proof

Create a TikTok/Reels content series featuring "Day in the Life" of community enterprise owners. User-generated content from travelers showing real interactions converts 4x better than branded content.

Impact: +40% reach among Gen Z travelers

üîç Consideration: Interactive Impact Calculator

Develop a "Trip Impact Calculator" on the website showing exactly how booking a specific tour benefits communities (e.g., "Your Peru trip = 3 months of income for a local family").

Impact: +25% conversion from site visitors

‚úÖ Decision: Simplify the Giving Journey

Add a "Round Up for Community" option at checkout on G Adventures, allowing travelers to donate the rounded-up difference. Micro-donations lower friction significantly.

Impact: +60% donor acquisition

üåç Experience: Digital Storytelling Kit

Provide travelers with a "Share Kit" including pre-written captions, hashtags, and photo prompts to encourage real-time social sharing during community visits.

Impact: +80% user-generated content

üì£ Advocacy: Alumni Ambassador Program

Create a formal ambassador program for returning travelers, offering exclusive updates, early access to new projects, and referral incentives for bringing friends.

Impact: +35% repeat engagement

ü§ù B2B: Partner Certification Badge

Develop a "Planeterra Verified Partner" badge for tour operators, with clear criteria and annual audits. Creates aspirational status and expands network beyond G Adventures.

Impact: +50% B2B partnerships
